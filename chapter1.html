chapter 1:

prevalent thoughts and the breathe

What is your most prevalent thought, and why?

What if we replaced that thought with the word 'breathe'?

Language is meant to convey the truth; truth that is not subject to change but truth that is eternally the same.
Among all my thoughts, which of them are more true than my breathe?

So we practice returning to to the truth, over and over again, looking forward to the moment when we no longer have to try so hard, in a world that can't stop pulling us away from truth.

Why?

Because we are too prideful to accept that life can be so simple.
"Breathe... that's it?"
We long for more, yet in our quest to build ourselves up so that we may be praised by others, we end up settling for less.

Friends, as we look forward to the moment when we no longer have to try so hard, we may also acknowledge that that moment has already come.

Now, we can breathe.

Breathe again.
